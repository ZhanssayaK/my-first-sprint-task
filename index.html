Conor speaks on Khabib in his recent interview

Conor has the unique ability to frame things to make people think the outcome will be different next time around. Even after getting KO’d five months ago, I watch this and find myself thinking “hmmm maybe Conor is going to make another title run.” Then when I sit and really digest what he’s saying, it feels like a lot of excuses and promises. Saying that last time was different and no more Mr Nice Guy, I’ll punch a hole through his head blah blah blah. Like… weren’t you trying to do that last time? I still think he lacks the edge he had at 145 and I still think Dustin takes this. And as far as the “no one wants to stand with me” kinda talk… yea no shit Conor. On fresh legs and a full gas tank in the 1st or 2nd round, no one is more dangerous. That’s why it’s mixed martial arts.
